Illuminate Your Shadow Side

Your shadow side is an opportunity to take a deep look at how “shadow aspects” influence your life and shape your choices. This is a step-by-step process designed to meaningfully work through your shadow aspects. It is a journey of healing and of becoming whole.

The Path to Wholeness Through the Shadow

Every person has a shadow side, and ignoring it condemns them to inner conflict. Harmony is possible only when we accept and integrate both sides.

A person can become whole and live in harmony with themselves only by recognizing and working through their opposing qualities.
